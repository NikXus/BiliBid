How BiliBid Works

A streamlined auction experience for both sellers and buyers — transparent, fast, and fair.

01
📦

List Your Item

Upload photos, set your starting bid, choose your auction duration — minutes, hours, or days. You're in control.

02
🏁

Buyers Compete

Buyers discover your item and place competitive bids. The live countdown creates excitement and drives prices up.

03
🏆

Highest Bid Wins

When the timer ends, the highest bidder automatically wins. No negotiations, no haggling — just fair competition.

04
🚚

Complete the Deal

Buyer pays, seller ships. BiliBid collects a small service fee only after a successful transaction.
